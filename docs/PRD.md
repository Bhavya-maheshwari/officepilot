# Product Requirements Document

## Product

OfficePilot

## Problem

Small businesses spend a large portion of their day performing repetitive administrative tasks.

Examples include:

- replying to emails
- searching contracts
- creating invoices
- organizing documents
- tracking customers

These activities reduce the time available for growing the business.

---

## Solution

OfficePilot provides an AI-powered operating system that centralizes business information and automates repetitive operational work using specialized AI agents.

---

## Target Users

Small businesses with 1–50 employees.

Examples:

- Marketing agencies
- Consultants
- Law firms
- Accounting firms
- Construction companies

---

## MVP Goals

Version 1 should allow users to:

- Register and log in
- Manage customers
- Upload business documents
- Chat with uploaded documents
- Generate invoices

---

## Success Criteria

A business owner can complete common operational tasks from a single application without switching between multiple tools.
