# OfficePilot

> Your AI employee for running a small business.

OfficePilot is an AI-powered business operating system that helps small businesses manage customers, documents, invoices and repetitive operational work using specialized AI agents.

---

## 🎯 Vision

Every small business should have access to an AI workforce capable of handling repetitive operational tasks.

Instead of hiring multiple assistants, OfficePilot provides specialized AI agents that work together to streamline daily business operations.

---

## 🚀 MVP Features

- Secure User Authentication
- Customer Management
- Document Upload
- AI Chat with Business Documents
- Invoice Generation
